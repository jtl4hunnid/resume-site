Pick a palette

Twelve sets, each shown on a real card so you can see the colors working together. Pick one and copy its three hex codes into the palette block in your page.

Every set here has been contrast-checked, so your text stays readable. Picking three colors that work together is a genuinely hard design problem. Choose from the list rather than inventing one.

How to use one

In your page, find this block near the top and replace the three values:

/* ============ PALETTE - EDIT THESE THREE ONLY ============ */
brand:  '#0C2559',
accent: '#2C62CC',
pop:    '#FFE019',
/* ============ END PALETTE ============ */

Leave ink alone. It sits outside the block on purpose, at a 17:1 contrast ratio, so your body text stays readable no matter which palette you pick.
